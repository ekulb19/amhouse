Executive Summary

American House is viewed as "blue-collar affordable" in some markets (Michigan) while operating premium properties in others (Florida, Somerset, Lakeshore). This creates market confusion and pricing tensions. The brand needs clarity on whether to modernize, how to position across diverse markets, and whether premium properties should remain under the American House umbrella.
